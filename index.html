Crea lo Que tu Quieras

Crea lo que tu quieras is a movement to remind yourself that you have the power to create your own reality. It’s about reaching deep inside to the passion that lights a fire no one can take away. The tenacity creatives have during their creative high is the fuel that makes you want to bring it all to life. Crea lo que tu quieras esta escrito en español, para darles crédito a los quienes me recuerda diario que yo tengo varios ejemplos en mi propia vida
